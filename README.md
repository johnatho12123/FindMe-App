# FindMe-App
Find Me is an instance of social discovery games, wherein individuals interact with people around them and play games to find each other. In Find Me, two players are matched based on their locations and then allowed to play a game where they attempt to find each other's location.The only way for them to do so is to exchange messages and give hints.

How FindMe Works

When you open FindMe the app looks for another player near you who is also playing. This other player is within 2.5 kilometers of you. When FindMe finds this player the game starts.

You do not know who this other player is. You do not know where they are. FindMe only gives you a hint. This hint is warmer or colder. It changes as you and the other player move around.

Your job is to use this hint and the chat to find the player before time runs out. You also have to use your instincts.

When you think you have found the player you hit the Found button. If you are right you win the game.

How to Play FindMe

1. Create Your Account

You sign up with your email. You pick your name, age and what you like. FindMe gives you an username. This username is different every time you play. It is like SwiftFox42 or PhantomLynx7. Nobody knows who you really are until the game is over.

2. Start the Hunt

The app finds your location. Then it puts you in the queue to find another player. It looks for players near you. If it does not find anyone it keeps looking. Just keep the app open.

3. The Hunt Begins

When FindMe matches you with another player a countdown starts. You have 10 minutes. You see a circle on your screen. This circle tells you how away the other player is.

FindMe gives you hints. These hints are:

away. You are more than 1.5 kilometers away from the other player.

Getting warmer. You are under 1.5 kilometers away from the player.

Warm. You are under 800 meters away from the player.

Hot. You are under 300 meters away from the player.

Found. You are under 80 meters away from the player.

The circle moves faster when you are close to the player.

4. Use the Chat

You can send messages to the player. You use your username. This way the other player does not know your name.

You can give hints. Ask questions. You can also try to confuse the player.

5. Walk and Play Fair

FindMe tracks how fast you are moving. If you are moving fast you get a warning. You have to play the game on foot. You cannot use a bike or a car.

6. Find the Other Player

If you find the player before time runs out you hit the Found button. You win the game. If time runs out the game is over.

7. Stay Safe

You can. Block the other player at any time. The game stops. A moderator checks what happened. FindMe never shares your location with the player.

The Rules of FindMe

You have to be at 13 years old to play FindMe.

You have to walk. You cannot use a vehicle.

You have 1 hour to find the player.

FindMe never shares your location.

You have to be nice in the chat. FindMe is a game. It is not a dating app.

Why Play FindMe

We spend a lot of time talking to people. FindMe is different. It starts online. Then you have to go outside. It is, like an experiment. You might make friends with the player.. You might become rivals. You will always have a story to tell.

FindMe wants you to get off your phone.. You have to use your phone to play.
